We look for team players who are willing to commit themselves to personal and team growth. It takes time to hone paddling techniques, and maintaining our paddlers’ physical, emotional, and mental well-being is crucial to the team’s success.

There are two mandatory water practices on Saturdays and Sundays; each water practice lasts for 1.5-2 hours. During weekdays, we expect all paddlers to attend conditioning on Wednesdays alongside two workout sessions in the gym.

We participate in one to two races every quarter. In the summer, there are more racing opportunities, both nationally and internationally. Paddlers must meet training requirements in order to race.

We practice on the water at Mother’s Beach in Long Beach. Rides to the practice sites will by car and are organized before each practice we have that weekend.

We will supply paddles and PFDs (life jackets) for you at our water practices. You are not required to know how to swim.
