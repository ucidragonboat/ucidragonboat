OUR CREW

Meet the incredible individuals who make up the UCI Elements dragon boat team! These dedicated students from the University of California, Irvine, bring to life the spirit of thunder, wind, and fire on and off the water. As the 22nd generation of UCI Elements, we stand on the shoulders of those who came before us, inheriting their legacy of determination and excellence. Together, we continue to push the limits, strive for greatness, and ensure that the UCI Elements dragon boat team remains a force to be reckoned with on the water and a source of inspiration for generations to come.
